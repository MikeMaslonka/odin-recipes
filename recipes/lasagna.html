Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is hearty, cheesy, and perfect for a family dinner. With layers of pasta, ricotta, and meat sauce, it's a comfort food favorite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the noodles and prepare the sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, cheese, and sauce in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes, then let cool before serving.
