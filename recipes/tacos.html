Tacos

Description

Tacos are a classic Mexican dish made with a corn or flour tortilla folded or rolled around a delicious filling. This version includes seasoned ground beef, fresh veggies, and cheese â€“ a perfect quick and tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 small taco shells (soft or hard)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream (optional)
  • Salsa (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Drain excess grease.
  3. Add taco seasoning and water to the beef; stir and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave.
  5. Spoon the beef mixture into the taco shells.
  6. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and any optional toppings.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
