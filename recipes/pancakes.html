Pancakes

Description

These fluffy pancakes are a breakfast favorite, perfect with maple syrup, butter, or fruit. They're quick to whip up and a comforting start to any morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form and the edges look set, then flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
